Routers

The NETGEAR Orbi seeks to revolutionize Wi-Fi connectivity – quite literally. Not only is it a strong contender for the best wireless router spot but also an innovative gadget. It is designed to stretch internet coverage further than any standard router.

In essence, the Orbi features two routers bundled into one, with the second working as a Wi-Fi extender. And the best part is that setting up the unit is effortless, a shift from the typical tedious set-up procedure or poor performance associated with most extenders.
